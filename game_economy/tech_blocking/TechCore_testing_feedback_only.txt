Made by C3BO in the spirit of MeowCatMeow’s economic system overhaul.
The TechCore patch relies on the infrastructure provided by Seth's TechBlocking modoption which he kindly allowed us to use for this public initiative.

Please read all the documentation on the above link. The tweak codes you require are there, just copy-paste straight into the lobby chat. 
***Due to the nature of the building provisionally used you must use the "Force load all units" tickbox at the bottom of the Cheat section.
Legion may also be used, worth testing those changes too.

The basis of this patch is the delay of higher tech tier timings through imposing a "research" cost. This is achieved through the construction of a building found on all T1Con's lab build menu. 
Once the team has achieved the required level of points, the team will permanently research the techlevel.

Keep the thread for actual testing feedback. Please include video links or replays attached to your comments so I can see the issue live and evaluate.
I intend to disregard unrelated noise but feel free to create additional ranting threads elsewhere.

Please let me know if anyone struggles with the instructions, been through them many times but I am happy to amend if anything needs clarification.
Feedback on testing parameters, unit/building costing and quality of gameplay is highly appreciated to allow us to fine tune the balance across all game modes..
